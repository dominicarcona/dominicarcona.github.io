Business Licensing Lead Prioritization

A US state tax agency sought to improve tax compliance among businesses selling physical products online to state residents.

  • Identified and acquired high-value external data sources, supplemented by custom web-scraped data, to construct a comprehensive training dataset of remote sellers.
  • Built and deployed regression models in Python to prioritize licensing leads by predicted gross revenue, balancing interpretability with predictive performance.
  • Communicated modeling decisions and results to non-technical stakeholders to maintain buy-in throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Pilot outreach generated over $1M per month in voluntary tax remittance within two years of launch.

Launching SME Lending Product

A top-five international bank planned to launch a small-business lending product for a targeted customer segment.

  • Supported end-to-end product design, including pricing, risk, fraud strategy, and first-year KPI roadmapping.
  • Led the statistical design of the pricing experiment, including statistical power calculations, test structure, and interpretation strategy for early post-launch decision making.
  • Designed an A/B price-testing framework to be deployed immediately at product launch, ensuring sufficient statistical power for early decision-making.
  • A three-tier pricing structure was selected, enabling rapid learning about optimal pricing while managing downside risk.

  • My project presentation earned top marks and was later used as an exemplar for future cohorts.

Business Licensing Lead Prioritization

A US state tax agency sought to improve tax compliance among businesses selling physical products online to state residents.

Technical highlights: Feature engineering on noisy web data 路 Interpretable regression modeling 路 Revenue-weighted prioritization 路 Stakeholder-facing diagnostics

My role: Led data acquisition and feature pipeline design, selected interpretable models over black-box alternatives to satisfy regulatory and stakeholder constraints, and communicated results to non-technical audiences.

  • Identified and acquired high-value external data sources, supplemented with custom web-scraped data, to construct a comprehensive training dataset.
  • Trained regression models in Python to prioritize licensing leads by predicted gross revenue.
  • Pilot outreach generated over $1M per month in voluntary tax remittance within two years.

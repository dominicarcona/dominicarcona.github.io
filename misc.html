Chess

Since rediscovering chess during the COVID-19 pandemic, I've continued to play and learn most days. I like to play chess online (mostly on chess.com) and occasionally in person (around Los Angeles). I follow top-level human tournaments as well as the computer chess championship. My philosophy is that I'll have more fun if I seize the initiative and play actively, even if it means playing from an objectively worse position more often. Explore my games and exploit my poor play with the links below.

Beach Volleyball

Coming from an indoor background in high school and college, I started playing beach doubles when I moved to Los Angeles (July 2021) and quickly fell in love with the game. Now entering my 6th season, I've worked my way up the CBVA ranks and earned my 'AAA' rating in May 2025. This year, I plan to compete regularly in open-level CBVA and AVP tournaments, and to keep improving as an athlete!

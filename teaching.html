Selected Student Profiles

I've had the privilege of working with a number of delightful students over the years. Here are some examples of students I've supported as a tutor.

Pre-med student underachieving in physics

Prompted by a 'C' on her first midterm exam, a pre-med undergraduate enrolled in calculus-based physics began working with me twice per week. Through targeted problem-solving strategies and conceptual explanations, she steadily gained confidence applying physics principles independently, aced her remaining exams, and earned a well-deserved 'A' in the course.

High school Algebra I student with a busy schedule

A motivated high school student balancing athletics and extracurricular commitments while taking Algebra I came to me for support. Our once-weekly sessions focused on efficient study strategies, foundational skill-building, and exam preparation. The student quickly improved his grades and reported reduced math-related anxiety.

Middle school student getting ahead in math

The parents of a mathematically gifted middle school student approached me seeking to enrich their son's math experience beyond the standard curriculum. Together, we explored advanced problem-solving techniques and previewed concepts from future courses in algebra and geometry. The student developed strong mathematical intuition and enthusiasm for the subject, setting him up for accelerated learning in high school.
